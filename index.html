04 / ALERT lymphoedema clinic Macquarie University Hospital · 2024 to present
Macquarie University Hospital Research Intern · continuing paper offer

Rebuilding a clinic's data pipeline, then running the study it unlocked.

I found the root-cause discrepancies in the clinic's weekly data-capture cycle and redesigned the process. That work cut documentation errors by 30 to 35% and lifted dataset completeness by 20%. Then I led the efficacy study on a novel lymphography protocol for personalised compression therapy. It earned the Judyth Sachs PACE Prize and a continuing offer on an upcoming clinical paper.
